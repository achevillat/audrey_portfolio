Website performance audit

Carrying out a performance test on your website allows you to identify elements such as loading speed, page accessibility and mobile friendliness that might impair the performance and/or user experience. This is what we use to optimise the marketing ability of a web page as it helps us identify the areas of the website that needs tweaking to increase your engagement and overall SEO ranking.

  • ◼ Complete website audit
  • ◼ Google analytics review
  • ◼ SEO ranking audit
  • ◼ Create a plan to correct the areas of weaknesses
  • ◼ Website optimisation
  • ◼ SEO strategy recommendations
  • ◼ Website usability and accessibility review
