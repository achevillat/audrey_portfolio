Logo Design

Your logo is a key part of your brand identity and is often the first thing your customers will see and remember. I can help you design a memorable logo that does the talking for you to make you stand out from the pack. The logo design is usually accompanied by a selection of brand assets that can be used alongside your logo depending on the context. We also recommend building your brand development strategy with us to ensure your identity lines up with your business objectives.
We can help you with:

  • ◼ Colour palette research
  • ◼ Competitor logo analysis and review
  • ◼ Moodboard
  • ◼ Review of the feelings/emotions we want the brand to convey
  • ◼ Logo creation
  • ◼ Icon design
  • ◼ Branding creation
  • ◼ Rebranding
  • ◼ Brand style guides
  • ◼ Brand and logo assets
  • ◼ Templates
  • ◼ Marketing collateral
