Who are we

Creafix Design is a digital freelance agency directed by me, Audrey! I am based in Christchurch, New Zealand and operate nationwide. For the last few years, we have worked with businesses around the Canterbury region across a range of industries. We work with small and medium businesses make the most of their digital presence to help them thrive. We are experts at building brand and digital marketing strategies alongside developing websites to convert your online visitors into customers.
We take time to get to know each business we work with to understand what make them who they are and what challenges they are facing. This helps us provide the best bespoke solutions for each unique business. This flexibility also means that we can take over your digital marketing completely on an on-going basis or simply land a hand when needed for a one-off project.
As a former engineer, it's probably no surprise that I like problem solving and efficient design. This is why we apply the KISS (acronym for "Keep It Simple, Stupid") principle which helps prioritising tasks and implement projects more efficiently. This helps our customers save time and money but also provides transparency to helps them understand what we are doing for them.

Why work with us

Whether you've been in business for 2 or 20 years, you need visual communication and strategy that will make you reach your ideal customers better and drive them to purchase your offering.
To keep the process simple, we like to start by reviewing the situation of your business and understand your goals and the challenges you may have. This allows us to work side by side to identify the potential areas of improvement to deliver the best action plan for maximising the effect of your digital tools.

  • ◼ One stop shop for a complete offer: all the services you need to maximise your digital marketing reach. You only have one agency to deal with, and one bill to pay.
