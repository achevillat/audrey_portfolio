Who am I?

French born engineer turned visual designer and front end developer who's happy to call New Zealand home.

I am passionate about design and digital technologies; I love to know how things work (hello engineering brain!) but also make things functional for users and pleasing to the eye (hello creative brain!). Being able to combine these two sides of my brain has been a revelation and encouraged me to start Creafix Design.
