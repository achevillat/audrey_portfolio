Who am I

Creafix Design is a digital freelance agency run by me, Audrey! I am based in Christchurch, New Zealand and operate nationwide. For the last few years, I have worked with businesses around the Canterbury region across a range of industries. I work with small and medium businesses make the most of their digital presence to help them thrive. I am an expert at building brand and digital marketing strategies alongside developing websites to convert your online visitors into customers.
I take time to get to know each business I work with to understand what make them who they are and what challenges they are facing. This helps me provide the best bespoke solutions for each unique business. This flexibility also means that I can take over your digital marketing completely on an on-going basis or simply land a hand when needed for a one-off project.
As a former engineer, it's probably no surprise that I like problem solving and efficient design. This is why I apply the KISS (acronym for "Keep It Simple, Stupid") principle which helps prioritising tasks and implement projects more efficiently. This helps my customers save time and money but also provides transparency to helps them understand what I am doing for them.
