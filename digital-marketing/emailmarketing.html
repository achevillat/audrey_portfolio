Email Marketing

Email marketing leverages the contact details of your existing customers, or prospects that have signed up on your website or landing page, to promote your products or services. It is often used to build a community around your brand. This part of your customer journey and allows you to stay front of mind with your customers via email without being overly intrusive. As a marketing agency, we can help you define the appropriate frequency of emails and content to share. Our goal is to bring your prospect back to your website to place an order or get in touch with you to purchase your services.
