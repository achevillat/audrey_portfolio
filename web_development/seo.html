Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

Search Engine optimisation or SEO is the process used to increase and improve the quality of the unpaid traffic coming to your website. The higher your website ranks on Google for a keyword, the more relevant and abundant the website traffic will be, therefore increasing your chances of sales conversion. SEO is an ongoing process that requires monitoring and adjusting on a regular basis to keep you ranking as high as possible on the Google search pages. We can help you create the strategy to get your website visible for your chosen keywords and report on key ranking parameters to adjust the strategy.
We can help you with:

  • ◼ Webpage design and content optimisation for Google ranking
  • ◼ Google analytics monitoring and reporting
  • ◼ Keyword selection and implementation
  • ◼ SEO strategy
  • ◼ SEO audits
